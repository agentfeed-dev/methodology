# The 23 Criteria

This document will contain the full definitions, scoring rubrics, and weights for the 23 criteria AgentFeed uses to evaluate agent frameworks.

**Coming in v0.2.**

The criteria fall into 5 categories:

1. **Isolation & Sandboxing** (memory, tool, multi-tenancy)
2. **Observability & Audit** (audit trail, drift detection, reproducibility)
3. **Reliability & Recovery** (failure containment, rollback, resource limits)
4. **Security & Trust** (input validation, exfiltration prevention, identity, cryptographic integrity)
5. **Operational Maturity** (patch cadence, supply chain, compliance, human-in-the-loop)
